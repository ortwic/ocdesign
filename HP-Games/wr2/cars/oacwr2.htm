Description

This is the designed Opel Astra Convertible for Mercedes Benz WorldRacing. After many requests I found some time to convert this car to WorldRacing 2. Of course it got a lot of new features. Falling of parts, possibilty to get scratches and dirt on the paint and a decent choice of different vinyls. Also the 3D-Model were improved and the car's performance was graded.
This car is perfect for sunny cruising on a winterly and snowy street by the side of cliffs. But you can also floor the gas pedal.
